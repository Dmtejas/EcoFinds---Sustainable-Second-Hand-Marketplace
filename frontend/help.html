Help & FAQs

Need assistance? Here are some common questions and helpful tips for using EcoFinds. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I list my items for sale?
    A: Log in, click "My Listings", and follow the prompts to add your item.
  • Q: How do I contact the buyer/seller?
    A: Use the messaging system on the platform after logging in.
  • Q: What payment methods are accepted?
    A: We accept credit/debit cards and popular digital wallets.
  • Q: Can I request a refund?
    A: Yes, follow the refund procedure available in the help section of your account.
  • Q: How do I track my order?
    A: Go to "My Listings" or "Orders" to view the status of your purchase or sale.
  • Q: How do I edit or remove a listing?
    A: Navigate to "My Listings", select the item, and click "Edit" or "Remove".
  • Q: How can I report a suspicious user or listing?
    A: Use the "Report" button on the user's profile or item page. Our team will review it promptly.
  • Q: Are there any fees for selling items?
    A: Some categories may have a small listing fee or commission. Check the fee schedule in your account settings.
  • Q: Can I negotiate the price with the seller?
    A: Yes, use the built-in messaging system to discuss offers directly with the seller.
  • Q: Is EcoFinds secure for transactions?
    A: Yes, all payments are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways.
