Sustainability, Health & Guidelines

At EcoFinds, we are committed to promoting eco-friendly consumption and ensuring the health and safety of our users. Please follow our guidelines for responsible trading and sustainable practices.

1. Sustainability Guidelines

  • Choose reusable or second-hand items whenever possible.
  • Recycle or repurpose products rather than disposing of them.
  • Check product condition and lifespan before listing or buying.
  • Support local sellers to reduce transportation emissions.

2. Health & Safety Guidelines

  • Ensure all electronics and appliances are fully functional.
  • Sanitize furniture, clothing, and personal items before use.
  • Follow safe payment and delivery practices.
  • Report unsafe or defective products to our support team.

3. Trading Etiquette

  • Provide accurate product descriptions and photos.
  • Respond to inquiries promptly and politely.
  • Meet in safe, public locations for exchanges.
  • Respect privacy and avoid sharing personal information unnecessarily.
